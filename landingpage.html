ACHIEVEMENTS


Thalappakatti Biriyani, originating from Dindigul, Tamil Nadu, boasts a unique blend of spices and tender meat, captivating taste buds since its inception in the 1950s. Its expansion beyond Tamil Nadu, both nationally and internationally, underscores its global appeal and culinary excellence. With numerous awards, cultural significance, and a strong social media presence, Thalappakatti Biriyani has become a cherished symbol of South Indian gastronomy, delighting food enthusiasts worldwide.
